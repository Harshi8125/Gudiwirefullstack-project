# ShieldGig 🚀

## 🌧️ Inspiration

This idea came from something we see very often. Whenever there is heavy rain or extreme weather, most people just stay home. But delivery partners don’t really have that option.

If they stop working, they lose their income for that day.

We realized this is unfair. These workers are essential, yet they have no protection when external conditions affect their work. That gap inspired us to build **ShieldGig**.

---

## 💡 What it does

ShieldGig is an **AI-powered insurance platform** designed to protect gig workers’ income.

Instead of workers manually applying for claims, the system:

* Automatically detects extreme conditions (rain, pollution)
* Triggers payouts instantly

---

## 🛠️ How we built it

For this phase, we focused on:

We designed:

* Weekly pricing model
